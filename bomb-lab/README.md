# Important Notice: Manual Target Distribution

For this lab, **targets are not pushed automatically**.

### How this works
- After you **accept the assignment**, the instructor will **manually push your individualized target** to this repository.
- This is intentional and required for the structure of the lab.

### Late Assignment Acceptance
If you accepted the assignment **after the initial target push window**, your repository may appear empty or incomplete.

In that case:
- **Email the instructor** with:
  - Your name
  - Your GitHub username
  - The lab name (Bomb Lab or Attack Lab)

Your target will then be added manually.

### Do not proceed until your target is present
If you do not see lab-specific files (e.g., binaries or target artifacts), **do not start the lab yet**. Contact the instructor first.

---

This manual process ensures academic integrity and individualized assessment.
